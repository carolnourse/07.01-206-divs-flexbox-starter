Hi, I'm Carol.

I'm a Responsive Designer & Developer who thrives on visual storytelling. When I'm not working, pursuing side projects or getting lost in the rabbit hole of blogs & books, I can be found travelling, learning languages and generally being a mad cat woman!

I was born and raised in East Anglia in the UK, but I spent several years living abroad in Greece and think of Crete as 'το νησί μου' - my island. I studied languages at university but didn't really find my language niche until I started learning Greek.
My passion: Well...it may come as no surprise but I love to travel. There is nothing as exhilarating as stepping through the airplane doors and into a new world and new life. The scents and sounds of another country are like an elixir to me.
A fun fact: I love creating mosaics! It first started in Crete, Greece when I joined a mosaic class. The joy of sitting there creating something and chatting when and if you wanted was the highlight of my week. My best mosaic is probably my Greek gekko.
